Privacy Policy

At BroTrading, protecting the privacy of our visitors is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy document explains the types of information that are collected and recorded by BroTrading and how we use it. By using this website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

When you visit BroTrading, certain information may be collected automatically. This includes your IP address, browser type, device information, operating system, referring pages, and the date and time of your visit. This information is used to analyze trends and improve the functionality of our website.

We may also collect personal information such as your name or email address if you voluntarily provide it through contact forms, newsletter subscriptions, or giveaway registrations.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect may be used for several purposes, including:

• Improving website performance and user experience
• Responding to inquiries and providing support
• Sending updates, promotions, or newsletters (if subscribed)
• Managing giveaways, events, or promotions
• Analyzing traffic and website usage

BroTrading participates in affiliate marketing programs. Some links on our website may be affiliate links, meaning we may earn a commission if users sign up or make a purchase through those links. These commissions help support the maintenance and development of the website.

Our affiliate partnerships do not influence our commitment to providing transparent comparisons and useful information for traders.

4. Cookies

BroTrading uses cookies to store information about visitors' preferences and to optimize the user experience. Cookies help us understand how users interact with our website and allow us to improve website functionality.

You may disable cookies through your browser settings. However, some features of the website may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

5. Analytics and Tracking

We may use analytics tools to understand how visitors interact with our website. These tools collect anonymous data such as page views, time spent on pages, and user interactions. This helps us improve our content and website performance.

6. Third-Party Websites

BroTrading may contain links to third-party websites such as proprietary trading firms or partner services. We are not responsible for the privacy policies or practices of these external websites. We recommend reviewing their policies before providing any personal information.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the security of your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Information

BroTrading does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that a child has provided personal information on our website, please contact us and we will promptly remove such information from our records.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised update date. Users are encouraged to review this policy periodically.
