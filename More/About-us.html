About BroTrading

BroTrading was created to help traders make smarter and more informed decisions when choosing a proprietary trading firm. With the rapid growth of prop firms in the trading industry, many traders struggle to find reliable information about evaluations, payouts, rules, and the best funding opportunities. BroTrading aims to simplify that process.

1. Our Mission

Our mission is to provide traders with clear, transparent, and easy-to-understand information about the best prop trading firms. We compare firms based on funding options, evaluation rules, profit splits, payout speed, and discounts so traders can quickly find the right firm for their trading style.

2. What We Do

BroTrading provides detailed comparisons, exclusive discounts, and resources for traders looking to get funded. Our platform helps traders explore different firms, understand their rules, and choose the best evaluation programs available in the market.

3. Prop Firm Comparisons

We analyze and compare top futures and trading prop firms so traders can easily see the differences between them. Our comparison tables highlight important factors such as profit split, evaluation duration, account sizes, and trading conditions.

4. Exclusive Deals & Discounts

BroTrading partners with leading prop firms to provide exclusive promo codes and discounts. Traders can access special offers and reduced evaluation fees through our platform.

5. Giveaways & Community

To support the trading community, we regularly run giveaways where traders can win funded account challenges and trading tools. We also aim to build a community where traders can learn, grow, and share experiences.

6. Transparency

Our goal is to keep the information on BroTrading transparent and helpful. While we may partner with prop firms through affiliate programs, our goal is always to provide honest comparisons and useful resources for traders.

7. Our Vision

We aim to become a trusted hub for prop trading resources — helping traders discover the best firms, access exclusive deals, and navigate the prop trading industry with confidence.
