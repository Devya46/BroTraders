What Traders Should Consider..

Asking the right questions helps avoid unnecessary risk and improves the chances of long-term trading success.

01

Is the Account Size Right for My Strategy?

Does the firm provide enough trading capital for your strategy, and are scaling opportunities available after consistent performance?

02

Do I Fully Understand the Profit Targets?

What profit target must be reached during evaluation, and are the rules realistic for your trading style and risk tolerance?

03

Are the Risk Rules Manageable?

Check daily loss limits, trailing drawdowns, and consistency rules to ensure they align with how you normally trade.

04

Are the Payout Rules Fair and Reliable?

Review payout frequency, profit splits, and withdrawal conditions to understand how and when traders receive their profits.

By evaluating these factors before starting an evaluation, traders can select a prop firm that fits their trading style and improves their chances of reaching a funded trading account.
